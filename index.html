What is Founder Therapy?

Founder Therapy is a confidential, peer-to-peer support event for startup founders. In small, moderated groups, founders share their most pressing challenges—from co-founder conflicts to funding fears—in a safe and supportive environment.

The format is built on the principle of sharing experiences, not giving unsolicited advice. This allows participants to gain credible perspectives from others who have walked a similar path, fostering resilience, building community, and providing actionable insights to navigate the turbulent journey of entrepreneurship.
